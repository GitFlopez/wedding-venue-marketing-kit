# Marketing Plan: Wedding Venue & Event Space Marketing Kit

## Market Opportunity

**US Wedding Industry:**
- $57.9B+ annual revenue (The Wedding Report 2024)
- 2.0M+ weddings/year in US
- Average wedding cost: $35,000 (national); $28,000 (Nevada)
- 235,000+ wedding venues and event spaces in US
- 95%+ are independent operators with no in-house marketing staff

**Las Vegas / Nevada Specifics:**
- Clark County issues 77,000+ marriage licenses/year — more than any other US county
- 500+ licensed wedding venues in Clark County
- Average Las Vegas destination wedding: $45,000+ (35% above national avg)
- March–June = peak booking season (spring + bachelorette season)
- Green Valley Ranch, Henderson, Summerlin = fastest-growing luxury venue corridors
- Nevada Catering Liquor Permit = compliance angle no generic AI addresses

**National High-Value Wedding Markets:**
- Nashville, TN: barn + country venues, bachelorette capital
- Scottsdale/Sedona, AZ: desert-chic, destination market
- Charleston, SC: historic plantation venues
- Napa Valley, CA: winery venues, $40K+ avg spend
- Aspen/Vail, CO: mountain luxury venues
