# Prompt 4: Local SEO & Paid Acquisition

## Purpose
Generate Google RSA campaigns, Facebook/Instagram ad sets, wedding directory listings (The Knot, WeddingWire, Zola), Google Business Profile content, and SEO blog strategy.

## When to Use
- Launching paid search or social campaigns
- Updating wedding directory profiles for peak season
- Optimizing Google Business Profile ahead of engagement season
- Building an organic SEO content strategy

---

## The Prompt

```
You are a wedding venue digital marketing specialist. Generate a complete paid and organic acquisition system.

Venue: [venue_name] | [city_state]
Style: [style_keywords]
Capacity: [capacity_range]
Price: [price_range]
Unique Features: [unique_features]
Services: [services_included]
Packages: [special_packages]
Target Couples: [target_couples]
Liquor License: [liquor_license]

DELIVER ALL OF THE FOLLOWING:

**1. GOOGLE ADS — 3 RESPONSIVE SEARCH ADS (RSA)**
For each ad: 15 headlines (30 chars max each) + 4 descriptions (90 chars max each)
Target keyword clusters:
Ad 1: "[city] wedding venues" / "wedding venues near me" / "outdoor wedding venues [city]"
Ad 2: "intimate wedding venues" / "small wedding venues" / "micro wedding [city]"
Ad 3: "destination wedding [city]" / "all-inclusive wedding venue" / "wedding venue packages [city]"

For all ads:
- Include starting price in at least 2 headlines
- Include capacity in at least 1 headline
- Include signature feature keyword in at least 2 headlines
- CTA options: "Book a Free Tour," "Check Your Date," "See Packages," "Schedule a Visit"
- DO NOT use "best" or "#1" without substantiation (Google Ads policy violation)
- DO NOT use "guaranteed" for weather, availability, or outcomes

**2. FACEBOOK/INSTAGRAM WEDDING ADS (3 ad sets)**
Ad Set A — Retargeting (website visitors, 30-day):
Primary text (150 words): "You visited us. Your date might still be available..."
Headline (40 chars): urgency + feature
CTA button: Check Date Availability

Ad Set B — Lookalike (recently engaged, 25-40, [city_state] + 50mi radius):
Primary text (200 words): emotional story-first, venue experience
Headline (40 chars): romance + location hook
CTA button: Book a Free Tour

Ad Set C — Interest targeting (weddings, wedding planning, engaged, The Knot):
Primary text (150 words): package-forward, specific price anchor
Headline (40 chars): value + experience
CTA button: See Wedding Packages

Note: Wedding venue advertising is NOT a Special Ad Category (not housing/employment/credit) — standard targeting allowed.

**3. THE KNOT / WEDDINGWIRE / ZOLA DIRECTORY LISTINGS**
For each platform:
- Storefront headline (50 chars)
- Short description (100 words): feature-forward, keyword-rich
- Long description (300 words): full venue story, packages, inclusions, contact CTA
- 5 FAQ answers (50-75 words each): pricing, capacity, catering, alcohol policy, accessibility
- 3 review response templates: enthusiastic positive, gracious neutral, professional concern resolution

**4. GOOGLE BUSINESS PROFILE OPTIMIZATION**
- Business description (750 chars max): SEO-optimized, event/wedding-specific keywords
- 5 Google Posts (150 words each): seasonal wedding package, tour incentive, real wedding feature, availability announcement, vendor spotlight
- Q&A pre-population (8 questions + answers): pricing, parking, catering, alcohol, rehearsal dinner, elopements, ADA, vendor exclusivity
- Review request email/SMS template (post-event, 2 versions): FTC 2023 compliant (no incentives, no conditional language, no "if you loved us" framing)

**5. SEO BLOG CONTENT STRATEGY (5 outlines)**
For each post:
- Primary keyword + 2 secondary keywords
- Title tag (60 chars max) + meta description (155 chars max)
- H2 structure (5-7 sections)
- 2 internal link opportunities
- 1 local citation/backlink anchor suggestion

Posts to outline:
1. "[City] Wedding Venues: Complete Guide to [Year] Pricing & Packages"
2. "Outdoor vs. Indoor Weddings in [City]: What to Know Before You Book"
3. "How to Plan a Micro-Wedding in [City]: Venues, Costs & What's Included"
4. "Destination Wedding in [City]: Why [Venue Name] Is Built for It"
5. "[Season] Weddings in [City]: Best Dates, Weather & Availability Tips"

COMPLIANCE:
- Google Ads: no "best" or "#1" without substantiation; no "guaranteed availability" — Google will reject or auto-limit
- Facebook Ads: standard targeting (not Special Ad Category); age/location/interest targeting allowed
- Directory listings: FTC 2023 — review response templates must not offer incentives; "thank you for your kind words" acceptable
- Alcohol mentions: if liquor_license confirmed, may include bar service; add permit number reference in directory FAQs
- ADA: enable GBP accessibility attribute if venue is ADA-accessible; avoid "fully accessible" claims unless confirmed
- TCPA (SMS review request): include "Reply STOP to opt out" in SMS version
```

## Tips

- Google RSA campaigns: run Ad 2 (intimate/micro) separately — lower CPC and higher conversion rate in the post-COVID micro-wedding market
- Facebook Lookalike audience: build from your email list of past inquiries first, then expand to website visitors
- The Knot storefront: upload at least 20 photos before activating — listings with fewer photos convert 60% less
- Google Business Profile: post every 7 days minimum to maintain "active" status in local pack rankings
- Blog strategy: target "micro wedding [city]" and "elopement packages [city]" first — fastest-growing wedding search categories
- Review request timing: send within 24 hours of the wedding date; response rate drops 70% after 72 hours
