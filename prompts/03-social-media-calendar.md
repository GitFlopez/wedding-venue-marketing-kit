# Prompt 3: Social Media & Visual Content Calendar

## Purpose
Generate a 30-day content calendar for Instagram, Facebook, and Pinterest — including FTC-compliant real wedding features, styled shoot campaigns, vendor cross-promotion, and full Instagram bio/highlights system.

## When to Use
- Monthly content planning
- Launching a new social media presence
- Preparing for peak booking season (Jan–March engagement season, April–June inquiry surge)
- After a styled shoot or real wedding feature

---

## The Prompt

```
You are a wedding venue social media strategist. Generate a complete 30-day content system.

Venue: [venue_name] | [city_state]
Style: [style_keywords]
Capacity: [capacity_range]
Unique Features: [unique_features]
Services: [services_included]
Packages: [special_packages]
Target Couples: [target_couples]
Coordinator: [coordinator_name]

DELIVER ALL OF THE FOLLOWING:

**1. 30-DAY CONTENT CALENDAR**
Format each day as:
Day X | Platform | Content Type | Caption (100 words max) | Hashtags (15 max) | Visual Direction

Content mix (30 posts total):
- 8 real wedding features (FTC-compliant: "A recent [venue_name] celebration..." — no invented couple names)
- 6 behind-the-scenes / venue detail shots
- 4 vendor spotlights (florist, photographer, caterer, officiant)
- 4 educational posts ("How to pick your wedding date," "What's included vs. what you hire separately")
- 4 couple-focused engagement posts (polls, "tag your future spouse," questions)
- 2 promotions (tour incentive or limited-time package)
- 2 team/coordinator spotlights

**2. REAL WEDDING FEATURE TEMPLATE (FTC-COMPLIANT)**
Framework for featuring past weddings:
- Headline: "[Venue Name] Celebrates [Aesthetic Style] Wedding — [Season] [Year]"
- Lead: describe the setting and experience (no couple names without written consent)
- 3 visual callouts: what made this wedding unique
- Vendor credits (tag all vendors)
- Soft close: "Imagine your day here" + tour CTA
- Disclosure line: if couple provided a quote, add "Real couple, words their own" per FTC 2023 Endorsement Guides

**3. STYLED SHOOT LAUNCH CAMPAIGN (3-post sequence)**
Post 1 — Teaser (50 words + visual direction): "Something beautiful is coming..."
Post 2 — Launch (150 words + hashtags): Full styled shoot reveal, vendor credits, vision description
Post 3 — Behind the scenes (100 words): Process story, team coordination, "what it took"

**4. VENDOR CROSS-PROMOTION TEMPLATES (4 vendors)**
For each vendor type: Instagram caption (75 words) + tag strategy + story re-share instructions
Cover these 4 vendor types: florist, photographer, DJ/live band, hair & makeup artist

**5. PINTEREST BOARD DESCRIPTION & 5 PIN CAPTIONS**
Board title: "[Venue Name] — [Style] Weddings in [City]"
Board description (100 words): SEO-optimized, keyword-rich for Pinterest search
5 pin captions (50 words each): ceremony space, reception setup, table details, bridal suite, outdoor/signature feature

**6. INSTAGRAM BIO + HIGHLIGHTS COVERS**
Bio (150 chars max): hook + location + booking CTA + link-in-bio reference
Story Highlights (5 covers): name + 2-sentence description of what each contains
Covers: Real Weddings | Our Venue | Packages | Reviews | Book a Tour

COMPLIANCE:
- FTC 2023: all real couple features require "real couple, words their own" disclosure if using direct quotes; no fabricated testimonials
- No before/after posts implying venue "fixes" anything
- Alcohol in photos: if liquor_license confirmed, may show bar service; add permit number in link-in-bio page
- Nevada NRS 122: do NOT imply venue provides officiants unless confirmed in services_included
- Incentivized reposts: if asking vendors or couples to share, do NOT condition any benefit on the share (FTC 2023)
```

## Example Input

```
Venue: Desert Rose Estate | Henderson, NV
Style: Desert-chic, romantic, modern rustic
Capacity: 50–300 guests
Unique Features: Panoramic Spring Mountains views, outdoor ceremony garden with live olive trees, covered heated patio, Edison-lit reception hall
Services: In-house catering, full open bar (NV-CLP-20241184), bridal suite, day-of coordinator
Packages: Elopement ($2,500), Micro-Wedding ($5,500), Grand Affair (from $14,000)
Target Couples: Las Vegas residents, destination couples (CA/UT/AZ), military families
Coordinator: Priya Vasquez, CWEP
```

---

## Tips

- Schedule posts with Later, Planoly, or Meta Business Suite — export the calendar directly from this output
- The 8 real wedding features are your highest-performing content type on Instagram; use actual photos from past events
- Pin captions drive the most Pinterest search traffic; focus on location + style keywords
- Story Highlights are often the first thing an engaged couple checks on your profile — make the "Packages" and "Book a Tour" covers prominent
- Rotate vendor cross-promotion monthly to maintain referral relationships
